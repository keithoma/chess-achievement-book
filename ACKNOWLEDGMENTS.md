# Acknowledgments

This project is made possible by the incredible generosity and technical excellence of the open-source chess community. In an era of closed-source software and paywalled data, these contributors provide the "Gold Standard" of chess tools for free.

### Stockfish
Special thanks to the **Stockfish Developers and Community**. Stockfish is not only the strongest chess engine in the world but also a triumph of collaborative open-source development. This project utilizes Stockfish for deep move evaluation and brilliancy detection.
* Website: [https://stockfishchess.org/](https://stockfishchess.org/)

### Lichess.org
Heartfelt gratitude to **Thibault Duplessis** and the entire **Lichess team**. Lichess provides the oxygen for modern chess development through their open API and public game databases. Their commitment to a "forever free" platform is the primary inspiration for this project.
* Website: [https://lichess.org/](https://lichess.org/)

### Norman Pollock (40H-PGN)
A massive thank you to **Norman Pollock**. His decades of meticulous work in cleaning, filtering, and organizing master-level OTB games into the 40H-PGN databases provide the essential "Mastery" benchmarks used in this tool's opening analysis.
* Website: [http://nk-qy.info/40h](http://nk-qy.info/40h)

### Niklas Fiekas (python-chess)
Thank you to **Niklas Fiekas** for creating and maintaining `python-chess`. It is the bedrock of almost every Python-based chess project in existence, providing a robust and expressive way to handle moves, PGNs, and engine communication.
* Source: [https://github.com/niklasf/python-chess](https://github.com/niklasf/python-chess)

### Eduard Nemeth (Solista)
Even though the current version of this tool utilizes human-centric master databases, I would like to specifically thank **Eduard Nemeth (Solista)**. His incredibly deep and high-quality "Solista" opening books were instrumental during the prototyping and testing phases of this engine runner.
* Website: [https://solistachess.jimdosite.com/](https://solistachess.jimdosite.com/)
